Game Rules

Objective: Align three of your symbols (X or O) horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
Number of Players: 2

Game Play:

1. Decide who will play with Xs and who with Os.
2. Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty square.
3. The first player to align three of their symbols wins the game.

End of Game:

The game ends when a player aligns three symbols, or all squares are filled without any alignment (resulting in a draw).
Play and have fun!
